# PCB Sponsorship & Manufacturing

The prototype PCBs for **PowerControlHub** were kindly supported by [PCBWay](https://www.pcbway.com).

From initial upload through to delivery, the process was straightforward and well handled. The engineering review helped catch potential manufacturing issues early, and communication throughout was clear and responsive.

The boards arrived well packaged and the overall build quality is excellent — clean silkscreen, solid solder mask, and good alignment across all layers. Assembly support was also smooth, with components placed accurately and ready for bring-up and testing.

This support has made it possible to move quickly from design to real-world testing, which is a big step forward for the project.

More updates (including testing results and build progress) will be shared as development continues.
